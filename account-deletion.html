DavisDiet – Account Deletion Request

If you are a user of the DavisDiet app and wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to customercare@davisdiet.com with the subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include your registered phone number in the email

Once your request is received, we will verify and permanently delete your account and related data within 7 working days.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Your full name
  • Phone number
  • Address and delivery details
  • Meal plan and order history

Retention Policy:

We do not retain your personal information after deletion. However, certain transaction or legal data may be retained as required by applicable laws.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the email above.
